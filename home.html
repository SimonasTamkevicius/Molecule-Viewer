Welcome to Molecule Viewer

Welcome to our Molecule Viewer homepage, where you can explore the fascinating world of molecules and their structures. Our website provides various tools to visualize and manipulate molecules with ease.

Our SDF Upload Page allows you to upload your own SDF files, which contain information about the structure and properties of various molecules. Once uploaded, you can view and manipulate these molecules in our viewer.

The Add/Remove Elements Page lets you customize the molecules you are viewing by adding or removing specific elements from the database. This is useful for creating new molecule altogether.

In the Molecule Selection and Display Page, you can select from uploaded molecules. Once selected, the molecule will be displayed in our viewer, where you can rotate and view them.
